Key Takeaways

01

HHS leads ESF #8, not FEMA. FEMA coordinates overall disaster response, but HHS has the medical expertise.

02

ESF #8 is an "alphabet soup" of agencies: CDC, ASPR, FDA, CMS, SAMHSA, NIH all play specific roles.

03

Support organizations like the Red Cross are crucial but aren't federal agencies - they're partners.

04

Federal response is supplemental - local and state resources respond first, federal fills gaps.
